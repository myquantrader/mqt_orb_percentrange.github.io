MQT_ORB_PercentRange

Opening Range Expansion in Percentage Terms

MQT_ORB_PercentRange shows how much of the full trading session’s range is created during a specific intraday window such as the Opening Range. Real‑time percentages and optional historical averages provide immediate volatility context.

How It Works

1

Track Two Windows

High/low values are recorded for intraday and full‑day windows.

2

Calculate Percentage

The intraday range is divided by the full session range.

3

Store History

Daily values are saved for rolling backtest averages.

4

Display Results

Results are shown as fixed text directly on the chart.

FAQ

Does this repaint?

No. All values are calculated on completed bars only.

Can I change the ORB times?

Yes. Both windows are fully configurable.

What does Backtest Days do?

It controls how many prior sessions are averaged.

Is this instrument‑specific?

No. It works on any market or timeframe.
